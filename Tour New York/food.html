Here Are Some Of The Foods You Have To Try!

Yummy Bagels!

Bagels At Absolute

Bagels are a staple in NYC but the Absolute Bagels on Broadway. The bagels here are freshly made everyday and even come with different types of toppings! The inside warm and chewy, the definition of a perfect New York Bagel.

At Absolute Bagel, they offer many types of cream cheese from Jalapeno Cream Cheese to a Walnut Raisin Cream Cheese. The variety you can find here is so wide. There is definitely a type of cream cheese to fit your taste buds.



Crisy Pizza!

Pizzas At Joe's

Everyone talks about how NYC pizza is the best and at Joe's Pizza they really prove it. Their pizza is just right amount, the right amount of cheese, the right amount of sauce, and the right amount of crunch. They took years to perfect their craft and boy is it good!

The best part about this store is that it closes at 4am so after a long night you can go out and enjoy a slice of warm, crunchy, delicious pizza!



fried chicken

bb.q Olive Chicken

Aside from the the classic NYC foods, there are some tasty cuisines from around the world. bb.q Olive Chicken takes a classic Korean tradition of chicken and beer and brings it to NYC! They have a wide variety of Korean food but their main focus is beer and chicken. Their chicken is fried twice and coated with Korean style sauces.

This place is best with a group! Eating chicken, drinking beer, talking to your friends, does life get better than this? I don't think so.



Fresh sushi!

Sushi Azabu

This underground bunker of a sushi restaurant is the perfect place to wait out an apocalypse, or to eat excellent sushi! The sushi served at Sushi Azabu is freshly delivered every morning and the chefs prepare it right when the customers order. Nothing is frozen here and you can really taste it. The place also gives a really cozy and intimate ambiance, perfect for a nice vacation date.

The price may be a little heavy but I promise it is worth it! Fresh fish prepared by a skilled chef in a cozy environment, where else can you go to find a place like this? I'll tell you: no where!



smokey meat!

O2 BBQ

This is a simple Korean BBQ restaurant with table grills and TV screens... EXCEPT IT'S ALL YOU CAN EAT!!! At O2 BBQ you can stuff your face with crazy amounts of delicious, seasoned meat and balance that with a little side of vegetables. This place is also self-cooked so you better brush up on your cooking skills before coming here!

O2 BBQ also serves sake! So who doesn't love to drink and eat until their stomach hurts?



Spicyyy kebabs!

Diwan Grill

For authentic Indian cuisine it doesn't get any better than Diwan Grill. This place offers a wide variety of indian food, from curry, biryani, tandoori, and other Indian standards. All seated in a cozy setting and friendly staff, you are guaranteed to love this place.
