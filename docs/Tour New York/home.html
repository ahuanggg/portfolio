Why You Should Visit New York City!

The 5 Boroughs

New York City isn't just Manhattan only, there are other great places to explore! NYC has places such as Williamsberg in Brooklyn for nice chill vibes, or Main Street in Queens for authentic Asian cuisine, or Bronx Zoo in Bronx to see some exotic animals. There are many places besides Manhattan that make visiting NYC worthwhile.

NYC's Famous Broadway Shows

New York City's broadway shows aren't famous for no reason. They are one of the best in the world- definitely a valid reason to visit New York City. There is a long 13 mile walk from Bowling Green to Inwood that has a whole bunch of broadway theaters. There is definitly something for everyone there!

NYC's Architecture

Buildings in New York City are famous all around the world! These include the Empire State Building, the One World Trade Center, the Flatiron Building, Rockefeller Plaze and many more! These buildings are definitely extraordinary and if you haven't seen them yet you have to see them now!

Delicious Food

Of course I had to add food! The food in NYC is impeccable- the pizza, the street vendor's hotdog, and I can't forget about the bacon, egg and cheese that really makes New York City, New York City. The food in New York City is fresh and plentiful. Since New York City is such a melting pot of cultures there are so many different foods that you have never tried!
