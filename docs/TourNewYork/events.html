Special Events

Christmas at Rockefeller

Christmas is always a wonderful time, Especially when you are in NYC. Every year they put up a big Christmas tree wiht beautiful lights and you can even ice skate infront of it. It surely is a surreal moment. It is definitely worth going here since it really is quite a scene. Especially when it's snowing you just feel so Christmasy. The Christmas lights are on by Decmeber 4th fomr 5:30am to 11:30pm so be sure to go when the lights are on!

Parades

Everyone knows that NYC throws the biggest parades. Any type of holiday you can name there is a parade. But the big one is usually Thanksgiving Day parade where they have big ballons of giant characters from Spiderman to even Pikachu! The parade route is quite long and a sight to see. The parade starts at 9:00am on 77th Street and runs until noon. If you wanna get the best spots you should arrive at around 6:30 am for the front row seats.

New Year's Eve

You have to see the ball drop if you come around New Years. It is a tradition! People travel far and wide just to see it because it really is that special. They start dropping it at 11:59pm and the ball drops slowly for 60 seconds, just enought time to make your new years resolutions. Police also block people coming in and going out at a certain point so make sure you plan accordingly!
